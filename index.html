2026 Season at a Glance

24

Grand Prix Races

The biggest calendar in F1 history, spanning from the Australian GP in March to the Abu Dhabi GP in December.

11

Teams on the Grid

Cadillac joins as the 11th constructor. This is the first new team to enter Formula 1 since Haas debuted in 2016.

New

Active Aerodynamics

Movable wing elements replace DRS. Every driver gets Straight Mode automatically, no gap to the car ahead required.

6

Sprint Weekends

Six events feature a Saturday Sprint race: China, Miami, Canada, Great Britain, Netherlands, and Singapore.

50%

Electric Power

The MGU-K was tripled to 350 kW, making each car's power unit roughly half electric, half combustion.
